About us

The school was started and founded by Olpejeta Conservancy with collaboration with CDF. The CDF sponsored the construction of several class ,and Olpejeta provided with books and also constructed the library ,with apparatus that are needed.The community assisted with cnstruction of dormitories and that is how the school came to be! The school participates in extra co-curriculum ;football,handball,netball,volleyball,table tennis,badmintton and the exciting co-curriculum was robotics which was introduced by olpejeta Conservancy to be specific, by the founder of Suitcase Company, Richard Nicole.
